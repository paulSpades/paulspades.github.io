SEISME is DJ Podcast focusing on electronic music - Break-beats, Drum&Bass, Electronic Funk and UK-Garage with a Romanian target audience, hosted by nowhitespace.

The podcast was initially going to be hosted on Mixcloud.com. As I was developing the branding and visual style, he decided to also have me develop a web app that hosts and streams the podcast. The app is soon to be live this summer, and this is one of the mockups for the UI.
